Refugees by Country of Origin from 2000 to 2015

For my Interactive Cartography & Geovisualization class, one of our first lab assignments was to use Leaflet and JQuery to map spatiotemporal information. I chose to look into refugee data in an attempt to visually trace large world events. Data was aquired from the Data World Bank.
Jasmin Becerra, Spring 2017

Push the temporal buttons, hover over symbols, and type into the search engine to explore the map!
